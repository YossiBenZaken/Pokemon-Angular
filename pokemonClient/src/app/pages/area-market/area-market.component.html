Premium packs

As soon as you buy a premium pack you become a premium member. You then have more options and advantages in the game!
Some examples are:
- More inbox and event space.
- More Race and Steal options.
- You can play the lucky wheel more than once a day.
- You can duel against each other.
- The time of the pokemon center is significantly lower.
- Your pokemon gain 5% extra EXP.
- And much more!
